Down South Louisiana Crawfish Boil

by Kim Hobley

Ingredients

  • 30-35 lb. sack of live crawfish
  • 6 oranges (cut into halves)
  • 6 lemons (cut into halves)
  • 1 stalk of celery
  • 6 bell peppers (quartered)
  • 12 peeled garlic cloves
  • 3 yellow onionss (quartered)
  • 3 purple onions (quartered)
  • One whole pineapple (chunked)
  • 1/2 gallon of pineapple juice
  • 4.5 lbs bag of Louisiana Crawfish, Shrimp, and Crab Boil Seasoning
  • Fresh Herbs: Thyme, Bay Leaves, Basil, and Oregano
  • 24 ears of frozen corn (mini or tot size)
  • 40 oz Budlight
  • 1 stick of unsalted butter
  • Lagniappe Ingredients can inlude: shrimp, potatoes, turkey necks, sausage, eggs and any other extra

Directions

I cook my boil in an outdoor 60qt Boiling Pot, with a cooking basket, so that I can cook the entire sack all at once, but use what you have readily available. The core ingredients and seasoning canbe used for at least one more sack of the crawfish.
